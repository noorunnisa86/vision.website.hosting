Commercial System:

A commercial solar energy system offers distributed generation, meaning the energy produced by your system can be used both at the point of generation and in nearby structures. Most commercial solar installation projects are connected to the local electrical grid so that you can take advantage of net metering. The efficiency of commercial solar panels typically ranges from 15% to 20%, and some can reach up to 25%.
